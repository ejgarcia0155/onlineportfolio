Project information

  • Project Period: 2023-TBD (In Development)
  • Platform: Windows PC
  • Made with: Unity for Development, GIMP for Design Elements and Assets
  • Developed first as a Project for Practicum but is still in further development as a personal project.

  • PART OF CS PRACTICUM OUTPUTS

    Game is Currently in Development

GAUNTLET BREAKERS is a round-based deck building FPS battle royale focused on completing bite sized stages to gain cards (that act as guns) with different effects or gain gold to buy cards to defeat and outsmart enemy players in a climactic duel.

GAUNTLET BREAKERS combine the addicting gameplay styles of deck building, Rogue-like gameplay and fast paced speedrun action to create a high octane Arena First-Person Shooter.
